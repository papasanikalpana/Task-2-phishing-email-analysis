# Phishing Email Analysis Report

## Sender Email
support@paypa1.com

Reason:
The sender uses "paypa1.com" instead of "paypal.com". This is email spoofing.

## Subject
Your Account Will Be Suspended

Reason:
Creates fear and urgency.

## Suspicious Link
hxxp://paypal.verify-login-security[.]com

Reason:
The website is not the official PayPal website.

## Greeting
Dear Customer

Reason:
A genuine company usually uses your real name.

## Urgent Language
"Failure to verify within 24 hours"

Reason:
Scammers pressure victims to act quickly.

## Conclusion

This email contains multiple phishing indicators:
- Fake sender
- Fake website
- Urgent message
- Generic greeting
- Email spoofing
